# Colors

### Primary

NRC orange is our main color. Although we consider the orange color one of our most important elements of the NRC brand, a tiny bit of restraint is warranted. Just painting everything orange is not an optimal way to showcase the NRC identity.

Themes serve as an organizational framework for color in NRC, with each theme based on a specific primary background color. There is one default “light” theme and one default “dark” theme.
